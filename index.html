a little about me

My name is Al Powell, and I'm a product and experience designer based out of Denver. With three years of consulting experience within the product development space and a recent graduate of University of Minnesota's UX/UI bootcamp, my career fulfillment has centered around working with businesses and individuals in unveiling their product and technology needs and leveraging my visual design and technical expertise to bring these ideas to life.

With the free time I have outside of the work grind, you can catch me training for my next long-distance running event, catching a concert at Red Rocks, brainstorming my next tattoo, or jumping around the globe capturing the essence of foreign cities and landscapes through photography.

Skillset

Discover

Through a user-centric lens, leverage design methodologies to understand and empathize for the end-users goals and pain points when it comes to the respective process or and technology they're working within. Methods learned include user flows, creating user-interview plans

Design

Applying a Atomic Design approach and a keen eye for Typography, Grids & Composition, Visual Hierarchy, convert user research into high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes with Figma to that are ready for handoff to the development team.

Development

Leverage HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, and Jquery to convert prototypes into scalable & rich web pages. Proficient understanding of object-oriented programming, API integrations, Github, and VSCode.

Projects

Government Agency RWD Case Study

Over a four week period as a UI Designer, reinvent a non-profit organization's website in order to improve involvement from the volunteer community. Driven by Atomic and User-Centered design, apply a cutting edge design & streamline paths of involvement to flesh out the non-profits overall mission and establish the connection between organization and volunteer community.
