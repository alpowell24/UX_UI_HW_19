My Iterative Approach

Discover

Leveraging a human-centered design philosophy, work with brands and businesses to address end-user requirements to be enabled and major pain points to be alleviated. Methodologies that are leveraged in this discovery phase include creating personas, defining user-stories, conducting competitive analyses, and determining the scope of work through a feature prioritization matrix.

Design

With the end-user requirements defined and prioritized, start fleshing out the solution design that fulfills the needs of the end-users from an experiential and visual standpoint. Key tools and methodologies applied within the design phase include process mapping, wireframing, usability testing, and leveraging an Atomic Design approach within Figma to fully flesh out high-fidelity mockups and prototypes.

Development

Once the high fidelity mockups and prototypes have beenvapproved with the respective brand or business, leverage technical expertise to address limitations and use cases that weren't already addressed prior to jumping into the build-out of the product design. Main skills and tools applied in this phase include HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, object-oriented programming, system integrations, VSCode, and Github.
